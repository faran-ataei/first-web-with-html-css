The Best Action Movies And Series of All Time

We count down the most explosive films in the history of cinema.

So along with the classic action fare, we've tried to invigorate this list with the best action anywhere you can find it. Action/comedy, sci-fi action, martial arts, superhero action, war, and adventure - they're all here.
